The Chef Food History

More than 25+ year of experience

Founded in 1898 in Algiers, Chef Food started as a small teahouse serving dim sum and other Cantonese dishes to algerienne people. It quickly gained a reputation for delicious and affordable food, becoming a favorite spot for locals and a social hub for the growing Algerienne community. Over the decades,Chef Food went through several renovations and ownership changes, but it always stayed true to its roots, focusing on authentic Cantonese cuisine.
Today, Chef Food is a Algerienne institution, known for its delicious food, rich history, and old-world charm. It attracts tourists and locals alike, eager to experience a piece of Algerienne culinary heritage.
